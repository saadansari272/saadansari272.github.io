Notable Projects

Performance Based Assessment of Existing Reinforced Concrete Bridges in Karachi

Undergraduate Final Year Design Project

During this project, twenty bridges across Karachi were initially assessed upon their visual condition. The bridges were then modelled and analysed using SAP2000 software to replicate the real-time loading on the sub-structure and super structure. It was found that most of the bridges are working at maximum load-bearing capacity and retrofitting measures may be needed sooner than later for critical bridges.
